HOW IT WORKS

Browse the small business LOGO SHOP to see if there is anything that takes your fancy. The small business logo is perfect for any business just starting up. Be it a Therapist, Life Coach, Yoga Instructor or Builder. All logos can be personalised with your name and company tagline. This is the perfect solution for anyone on a tight budget.

To personalise your logo, all you have to do is let me know your business name and tagline if you have one. Your logo will come with full colour codes and several file formats suitable for Digital, i.e. Social Media and Print use. A full explanation of file types will be given.
